Teaching

Tom's current and past taught modules: Econometrics (BSc Economics); Economics of Health (MSc Economics); Applied Health Economics (PhD and MSc in Health Economics and Policy); Data Analysis (PhD and MSc in Health Economics and Policy).

PhD supervision

Tom currently supervises PhD students on a range of topics across his research expertise, including estimating the effects of AI-assisted diagnosis in breast cancer screening, the impacts of admission to hospital at the weekend or weeknight for emergency care outcomes, the productivity impacts of the adoption of scribes in secondary care, and the effects of COVID-19 on inequalities in cancer care in England. He is happy to accept enquiries for PhD study on questions across his research interests.
